Fun Things to do in the Cuse!

Destiny USA

More than just a mall, Destiny USA is a shopping and entertainment destination. You can spend hours browsing popular stores like Apple, Nordstrom Rack, and H&M, but what makes it stand out are the activities. Challenge your friends to a game at 5 Wits, an immersive escape room experience, or try indoor go-kart racing at RPM Raceway. Afterward, catch a movie at the Regal Cinema IMAX Theater, or grab a bite to eat at the Canyon Climb Adventure, a ropes course towering 70 feet in the air!

Erie Canal Museum

Learn about the history that shaped Syracuse at the Erie Canal Museum, located in a 19th-century Weighlock Building. Explore interactive exhibits that tell the story of the Erie Canal and how it transformed the economy of not just Syracuse, but the entire state. Don’t miss the full-sized canal boat and the fascinating model of the canal as it was in its prime.

Onondaga Lake Park

Dubbed the “Central Park of Central New York,” Onondaga Lake Park is a 7.5-mile stretch of outdoor beauty along the lakeshore. Rent a bike or go for a leisurely walk while taking in the scenic views of the lake. You’ll also find playgrounds, dog parks, and the Salt Museum, which provides insight into the region’s “salt boom” history. On warm days, rent a kayak or canoe and get out on the water!

Syracuse University

For a mix of history, architecture, and university spirit, a visit to Syracuse University is a must. Take a self-guided tour around the Hall of Languages, one of the oldest buildings on campus, which offers sweeping views of the city. Sports fans can check out JMA Dome, the largest domed stadium on any U.S. college campus, where you can catch a football or basketball game depending on the season.

Everson Museum

Art enthusiasts will love exploring the Everson Museum, designed by world-renowned architect I.M. Pei. This museum features an impressive collection of American ceramics and paintings. With rotating exhibitions, you’ll find modern and contemporary works that span photography, sculpture, and video. The museum also offers interactive workshops and film screenings, making it a dynamic stop for anyone interested in the arts.
