Destiny USA

More than just a mall, Destiny USA is a shopping and entertainment destination. Challenge your friends to a game at 5 Wits, try indoor go-kart racing at RPM Raceway, or enjoy a movie at the Regal Cinema IMAX Theater!

Erie Canal Museum

Learn about the history of Syracuse at the Erie Canal Museum, housed in a 19th-century Weighlock Building. Don’t miss the full-sized canal boat and model of the canal in its prime.

Onondaga Lake Park

Onondaga Lake Park is a 7.5-mile stretch of outdoor beauty. Rent a bike, enjoy a scenic walk, or get out on the water with a kayak or canoe.

Syracuse University

Visit Syracuse University for its architecture, history, and spirit. Don’t miss the Hall of Languages or a game at the JMA Dome!

Everson Museum

Art enthusiasts will enjoy the Everson Museum, featuring American ceramics, paintings, and rotating exhibitions. Perfect for modern and contemporary art lovers.
