Dinosaur Bar-B-Que

Dinosaur Bar-B-Que serves some of the best slow-cooked BBQ in the country. Known for its bold, smoky flavors, the pulled pork, brisket, and St. Louis-style ribs are must-tries. Pair them with classic sides like mac and cheese or BBQ beans for an authentic experience!

Pastabilities

Pastabilities is famous for its house-made pastas and signature Hot Tomato Oil. Enjoy artisanal dishes like Chicken Riggies or Duck Bacon Flatbread in a cozy and rustic setting. Perfect for both casual meals and special occasions!

Apizza Regionale

Apizza Regionale is a haven for wood-fired pizza lovers. Savor the Margherita for a taste of Naples or try the Pepperoni and Hot Honey pizza. With locally sourced ingredients, every bite is a farm-to-table delight!

Funk 'n Waffles

Funk 'n Waffles offers an eclectic mix of savory and sweet waffles. From the Jive Turkey to the Banana Bread Waffle, there's something for everyone. Add live music to the mix for an unforgettable dining experience!
